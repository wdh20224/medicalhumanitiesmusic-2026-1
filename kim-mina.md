# Rain Man

The film Rain Man portrays a character named Raymond who has Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD). He shows difficulties in social communication, repetitive behaviors, and hypersensitivity to sensory stimuli. At the same time, he possesses exceptional memory and calculation abilities, often associated with Savant Syndrome. The film presents autism not only as a limitation but also as a unique cognitive condition.
